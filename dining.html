The Overlook Cafe

The Overlook Cafe is a 4-star rated restaurant offering sumptuous dining in an informal and cozy setting featuring the culinary artistry of our very own master chef, Julian Ivers. Pearched upon a granite outcropping atop historic Whistler's Peak, the restaurant's spectacular scenic vistas of the Northern California coast-line provide an ambiance truly unique to the Pacific Trails Resort dining experience.
  • Exquisite dining prepared in a health-conscious way
  • Finest locally-sourced, organic, non-GMO ingredients
  • From the simple classics to elegant haute cuisine
  • Full-service bar with extensive wine/scotch selections
  • Varied menus featuring local seasonal specialties
  • Indoor seating or dining on our veranda overlook.

  • Breakfast: 6:30am - 10:00am
  • Lunch:       11:30am - 3:00pm
  • Dinner:      5:00pm - 10:00pm
