The Lodge at Pacific Trails

The Lodge is the central hub for your activities during your stay at Pacific Trails. It's a great place to meet, socialize, exercise or just have fun. There are all kinds of unique and special places to explore here. You can take in a good book at Sophie's Retreat, work out in our fully-outfitted health club, participate in daily yoga classes, have a cocktail at Woody's Getaway with some new friends, or shoot a round of billiards in The Cue Room. And the outdoor pool and spa offer a nice soothing break from the rigors of hiking the trails or paddling along the coast.
  • World-class locker room and spa facilities
  • Fully equipped exercise and work-out rooms
  • Heated outdoor pool and spa on the veranda
  • Woody's Getaway Lounge (11:00am - 1:00am)
  • Quiet reading and contemplation in Sofie's Retreat
  • Billiards and card games in the The Cue Room
  • Communications center and concierge services
  • Gifts and sundries from our gift shop
