The Yurts at Pacific Trails Resort

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.
How are the yurts furnished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. An alcove with a loo and sink with hot and cold running water help to enhance your luxury camping experience. Full shower and restroom facilities are located inside the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring a sense of adventure and some time to relax! Most guests also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan for the chance of changing weather by dressing with more than one layer of clothing.
